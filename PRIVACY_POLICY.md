# Privacy Policy for Camera Kit

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview
Camera Kit is a desktop application that applies Snapchat lenses to your camera feed.

## Data Collection
- **Camera Access:** The app accesses your webcam to display video with AR lenses
- **Local Storage:** Camera selection preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Data Transmission:** No video, images, or personal data is sent to external servers

## Data Usage
- Camera feed is processed locally on your device in real-time
- No recordings are made
- No data is uploaded or shared with third parties
- Camera settings are saved locally for convenience

## Third-Party Services
- **Snap Camera Kit SDK:** Used to apply AR lenses to camera feed
- Snap's services process camera data locally according to their privacy policy: https://snap.com/privacy/privacy-policy

## Data Storage
- All data remains on your local device
- No cloud storage or remote servers are used
- You can clear stored preferences at any time

## Your Rights
- You can revoke camera permissions at any time through Windows settings
- You can uninstall the app to remove all local data
